Compact, yet mighty; ready to push your amp to 11 and beyond, while providing sublime, always-on buffering to maintain and cleanse your signal.

The Booffer Buster is a swiss army knife for musicians needing good preamplification, buffering, clean and dirty boosting, or all of the above!

With a buffer circuit channeling Pete Cornish, featuring a low noise floor and active RF filtering to ensure signal integrity, the Booffer Buster can save your tone from any kind of loss accosciated with long cable runs, funky sound sources and/or strange pedal chains.

The boost circuit is true bypass switchable post-buffer, and is modelled after classic tube power amp designs using JFET emulation. The result is an nearly-transparent boost (with a hint of compression) on low gain settings, and sutble, but rich overdrive on high gain settings.
